About Us

Welcome to Realcare Health Services Limited, your trusted partner in homecare and supported living services in Dagenham, London, and beyond.

Mission Statement:

At Realcare Health Services, our offering extends beyond mere accommodation; it’s a nurturing home environment where individuals can foster their independence in safety. Our exceptional team of caregivers provides round-the-clock support to cultivate a positive and secure atmosphere.

Our Values:

Dignity, respect, and equality are the cornerstones of our approach. We believe that every individual deserves to be treated with compassion and understanding, we specialise in supporting with learning disabilities and complex needs, including severe autism, asperger's syndrome ADHD, and behavioral challenges.

Our Supported Living In Services Includes

High-Quality Accommodation

A well-maintained and comfortable living environment, decorated and furnished to high standards. This is achieved with the help of an in-house maintenance team that ensures a safe and pleasant home for all residents.

Independent Living Programs

Personalized programs designed to help individuals develop the skills needed for independent living. Each program is tailored to meet the unique goals and needs of each resident.

24/7 Support and Year-Round Availability

Around-the-clock support staff is available every day of the year, offering reliable assistance for residents whenever they need it, day or night.

Customizable Care Packages

Flexible care packages are created to meet the specific needs of each individual, with varying levels of support that can be adjusted as needed.

Health and Well-being Support

Assistance with registering for local health services and managing personal healthcare, ensuring access to resources and support for maintaining good health.

Education, Employment, and Training Assistance

Guidance and support in exploring education opportunities, employment, and training, helping residents gain skills and pursue career goals.

Future Housing Support

Help with planning and securing future housing arrangements, including information and support for finding and transitioning to permanent accommodation.

Counseling and Specialized Support

Access to counseling services for a range of needs, including substance abuse, mental health, sexual health, anger management, and safeguarding, tailored to each individual’s circumstances.

Financial Support and Advice

Financial guidance, budgeting help, and assistance in managing finances, tailored to help individuals build financial independence and stability.

Advocacy and Peer Support

Access to advocacy services, peer support groups, and organizations like the Care Leavers Association, fostering a sense of community and providing resources for personal growth and social connection.
